Reader Study

This is a research project. We will first ask a few brief survey questions. Then, we will show you OCT reports and ask you to rate the likelihood of glaucomatous visual field defect (GVFD) from these reports. Each case presents one image for independent assessment. Please use your allocated Reader ID.

All submissions are collected anonymously for research purposes using unique reader identifiers. For questions, please see contact details below. Participation is voluntary and you can withdraw from the study at any point by contacting the investigator below.

All responses are saved automatically, but as a back-up, could you export your responses for each session as an Excel file, please, and provide to the investigator below at the end of your session.

Your unique ARGUS ID will be provided upon registration. No password is required. Please make a note of your ARGUS ID, you will need it for the login page.

Having trouble? Please contact Dr Y Scantling-Birch if issues with access.
